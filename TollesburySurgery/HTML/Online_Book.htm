APPOINTMENT SYSTEM - ONLINE APPOINTMENTS

We offer a small selection of our appointments to be booked online via the internet. These appointments are for GPs only and can be booked up to 6weeks in advance. You may only book a maximum of two appointments at any one time via the internet. To register please present a photographic identification at reception who will generate you your own private log-in details. Please refer to separate heading.

OPEN CLINIC

Our GPs morning surgeries are open access and start at 09.00am. These are not pre bookable. Anyone who turns up before 10.30am will be seen, but remember: these surgeries are very popular and sometimes there can be a wait. It does, however, mean that you can get to see a GP at short notice. The doors open at 8am with clinic starting at 9am.

APPOINTMENT REMINDER SERVICE

An appointment reminder service in now in place to confirm your appointment at the surgery by SMS text message to your mobile phone. This is to help patients remember and reduce the “Did not attend” appointments which are wasted. A confirmation text will be sent to your mobile at the time of booking your appointment and a reminder is sent 09:00 the day before your appointment.

To book an appointment with the nurse please note the nurses are a multi skilled team and offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of the patients. When booking an appointment you will be asked for a brief description of the problem. This is to ensure you are given an appointment with the right person at the right time. Nurses skills are listed below:

  • Blood test
  • Blood Pressure
  • Cervical Screening
  • Dressings
  • ECG
  • Ear
  • Family Planning
  • Immunisation
  • Minor illness or injury
  • NHS/Senior Health Checks
  • Review of Asthma
  • Review of Diabetes
  • Smoking Cessation
  • Midwife- offering maternity Service.
