To book an appointment with the doctors please click here.

To book an appointment with the nurse please note the nurses are multi skilled team and offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of the patients. When booking an appointment you will be asked for a brief description of the problem. This is ensure you are given an appointment with the right person at the right time. Nurses skills are listed below:

  • Blood test
  • Blood Pressure
  • Cervical Screening
  • Dressings
  • ECG
  • Ear
  • Family Planning
  • Immunisation
  • Minor illness or injury
  • NHS/Senior Health Checks
  • Review of Asthma
  • Review of Diabetes
  • Smoking Cessation
  • Midwife- offering maternity Service.
