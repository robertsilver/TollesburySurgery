Online Prescriptions

To request your prescriptions, please click here

Please note, that for those under 18 years of age, we do not issue user name and password.

Please give us two working days notice when requesting a repeat prescription. Try not to run out of your medication or demand a prescription immediately as this will often involve interrupting a doctor who might be seeing other patients. You can order your medication up to 14 days before it is due, to allow you to manage holiday periods only. Please put in the notes box to explain why you are ordering your medications early.

The current system of allowing prescriptions to be emailed in to the surgery has now ended. Any prescriptions which are sent to our old email prescription service will not be processed.

Electronic Prescription Services

The big prescription switchover came to Tollesbury Surgery on the 8th January 2014. We have switched to the new NHS Electronic Prescription Service.

What is the new NHS Electronic Prescription Service - it means that your doctor will no longer give you a paper prescription. Instead, an electronic prescription will go straight to the Pharmacy you choose. We want getting your prescription medicines to be as easy as possible, so to nominate the Pharmacy of your choose just speak to any member of Tollesbury Surgery Team or Pharmacy team today.
