Dispensing

Our dispensary is open during all our opening hours. By law, patients living within 1.6km of the surgery may not have their prescription dispensed by our pharmacy. If you have any queries about this, please ask at reception. Please give 48 hours notice for a repeat prescription. Repeats ordered on a Friday will be ready for collection the following Tuesday. We do not take repeat prescriptions by telephone, except in extenuating circumstances. Requests for repeats may be brought to either the Tollesbury or Tolleshunt D'Arcy surgery, faxed on 01621-869023, or emailed to, prescriptions.f81076@nhs.net
